How to find us:

By car:

Via the A 27, take the exit Bremerhaven Mitte, continue on the Grimsbystraße, continue on Lloydstarße, turn left into the Columbusstraße. The hotel is on the left.

If available, you are welcome to use the hotel's own parking garage (€ 16.00 per day). Additional parking is available nearby.

By public transport:

Stop is "Bremerhaven central station".

From there you can reach us in 10 minutes by taxi or you can use the bus lines 501, 502, 505, 506, 509.
